FedRAMP Authorization Process

If you have a Cloud Service Offering (CSO) that is in use by the federal government, you should be thinking about obtaining a FedRAMP authorization. There are two ways to authorize a cloud service through FedRAMP: through individual agencies and the Joint Authorization Board (JAB). Click within the below diagram to learn more.
