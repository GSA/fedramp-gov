Understanding the transition from Rev. 4 to Rev. 5

The FedRAMP Joint Authorization Board has approved the FedRAMP Rev. 5 baselines! The FedRAMP baselines were updated to correspond with the National Institute of Standards and Technologyâ€™s (NIST) Special Publication (SP) 800-53 Rev. 5 Catalog of Security and Privacy Controls for Information Systems and Organizations and SP 800-53B Control Baselines for Information Systems and Organizations.

Rev. 5 documents can be found on the Documents and Templates page.

Open Security Controls Assessment Language (OSCAL) Templates can be found in the OSCAL GitHub.
