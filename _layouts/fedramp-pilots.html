Piloting Progress: Streamlining, Innovating, Securing

Explore opportunities to make a difference in improving FedRAMP by participating in the FedRAMP Pilot Program.

Making FedRAMP better is a team effort! As we modernize, we have to engage and innovate with stakeholders. To help us grow and scale the program, we are inviting cloud service providers, federal agencies, and other interested stakeholders to apply to participate in our new pilot program. Our aim is to use learnings from these pilots to refine our approach, improve processes, and explore new ways to deliver modern, secure cloud services across government. FedRAMP’s pilots will focus on innovations that benefit participants and the program as a whole, from improving the experience of cloud service providers considering FedRAMP, to making it easier for agencies to leverage market products in a secure way.

To make this program a success, we are keeping three things in mind:

Ensuring that we are considering all stakeholder points of view.

To achieve this, our aim is to include partners across agencies, academia, industry, and internal teams in the pilot process.

Making a measurable, positive difference in FedRAMP’s operations.

Our pilot programs will start with a defined transformative hypothesis to test and the anticipated results for our stakeholders.

Increasing collaboration between FedRAMP and stakeholders.

Communication between participants and the program during the pilot testing period will be key, and participants will regularly share processes, best practices, and successful examples to improve program effectiveness and turn the pilot experience into actualized change.

As a pilot participant, you will have the opportunity to:

  • collaborate on projects that work to design, refine, and deliver a more streamlined approach to security and FedRAMP processes.
  • identify ways to reduce the costs of getting or staying authorized
  • improve the experience of agencies and cloud service providers reuseing offerings
  • explore and showcase innovative cybersecurity approaches

Participants’ varying expertise and points of view will be vital in improving the accessibility of the market to cloud service providers and making sure agencies have the most up-to-date technology to help government work better for everyone.

We invite stakeholders to explore current pilot opportunities to participate. Stay informed about future pilot opportunities by joining our mailing list and be the first to know when new pilots are announced.
