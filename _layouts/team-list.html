The FedRAMP Team

The team is dedicated to advancing the adoption of secure cloud technologies within government agencies, strengthening the framework for a secure authorization of these technologies, and fostering strong partnerships with FedRAMP stakeholders. The office is structured into three primary branches: the Office of the Director, the FedRAMP Authorization Branch, and the FedRAMP Experience Branch. Each branch operates with distinct goals and priorities, working in alignment with the program's overarching mission to ensure the secure and efficient use and reuse of the latest cloud technologies in government operations.
